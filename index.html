JOKERZ 31

This premium strain is an indica-dominant cross of the ever-popular White Runtz and Jet Fuel Gelato.

Euphoric and dreamy at first, with tingling mental energy that makes you giggle, before giving you a head-to-toe body high. This strain has a sweet, creamy taste with gassy funk, pine, and fruity undertones.
Jokerz flowers into silver and green frosty buds.

Jokerz took first place in the 2022 Leaf Bowl in Oregon.
